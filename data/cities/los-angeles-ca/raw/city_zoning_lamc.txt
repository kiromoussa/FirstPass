CITY / MUNICIPAL CODE — Los Angeles, CA
Los Angeles Municipal Code §12.22 A.33 — Accessory Dwelling Units (zoning layer)
Representative excerpts compiled by the Municipal Code Researcher agent.

### LAMC §12.22 A.33(c) — Unit Size [S1]
A detached accessory dwelling unit shall not exceed 1,200 square feet of floor
area. An ADU of up to 800 square feet is permitted by right on any lot regardless
of floor-area-ratio, lot coverage, or open-space standards. Floor area is measured
to the interior face of the exterior walls.

### LAMC §12.22 A.33(d) — Side Setback [S1]
A detached accessory dwelling unit shall provide a side yard setback of not less
than 4 feet. No setback is required for a legally existing permitted structure
that is converted to an ADU, or for a new ADU built in the same footprint.

### LAMC §12.22 A.33(d) — Rear Setback [S1]
A detached accessory dwelling unit shall provide a rear yard setback of not less
than 4 feet measured from the property line to the nearest wall of the unit.

### LAMC §12.22 A.33(e) — Parking [S1]
One off-street parking space is required per accessory dwelling unit. This
requirement is waived where the ADU is within one-half mile walking distance of
public transit, within an architecturally and historically significant district,
or where on-street permit parking is required but not offered to the ADU.

### LA City Planning — ADU Submittal Requirements [S4]
A complete ADU application must include a plot plan drawn to scale, floor plans,
building elevations for all sides, and a Title-24 energy compliance report.
Applications missing any required sheet are deemed incomplete and are not routed
for plan check.
