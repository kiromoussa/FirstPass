COUNTY CODE — Los Angeles County (unincorporated areas)
LA County Code Title 22 (Planning & Zoning) — Accessory Dwelling Units
Representative excerpts compiled by the County Code Researcher agent.
Note: applies to parcels in unincorporated LA County, not within the City of LA.

### LA County Code §22.140.640 — ADU Development Standards [S3]
In unincorporated Los Angeles County, a detached accessory dwelling unit shall
not exceed 1,200 square feet and shall maintain side and rear setbacks of not less
than 4 feet. ADUs are permitted in any zone that allows a single-family or
multifamily dwelling.

### LA County Code §22.140.640 — Height [S3]
A detached accessory dwelling unit in unincorporated County territory is limited
to 16 feet, increased to 18 feet within one-half mile of a major transit stop,
consistent with state law.

### LA County Code Title 26 — Building Code Adoption [S3]
The County adopts the California Building Standards Code (Title 24) with local
amendments. Construction of an accessory dwelling unit must comply with the
adopted building, residential, plumbing, mechanical, electrical, and green
building standards as amended by the County.
