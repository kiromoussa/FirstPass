PLUMBING CODE — California Plumbing Code (CPC), Title 24 Part 5
Representative excerpts compiled by the Plumbing Code Researcher agent.

### CPC §401 — Minimum Plumbing Fixtures per Dwelling Unit [S7]
Each dwelling unit, including an accessory dwelling unit, shall be provided with a
kitchen sink, a water closet, a lavatory, and a bathtub or shower. Each required
fixture shall be connected to an approved water supply and drainage system.

### CPC §501 — Water Heater Requirements [S7]
Each dwelling unit shall be served by a water heater of adequate capacity.
Storage water heaters shall be anchored or strapped at the upper and lower
one-third of the appliance to resist seismic displacement, and shall discharge
through an approved temperature-and-pressure relief valve to an approved location.

### CPC §422 — Fixture Count and Accessibility [S7]
The number and type of plumbing fixtures shall be based on the occupancy and
number of occupants. Fixtures shall be spaced to provide the clearances required
for use and cleaning.
